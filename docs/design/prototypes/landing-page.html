FAQ

Answer the questions that block conversion.

Keep these answers operational. No speculative promises and no large policy wall.

Do I need a membership to book classes?

Yes. Browsing starts publicly, but class booking only opens once your membership is active.

Can I compare plans before creating an account?

Yes. The landing page shows plan pricing, duration, and booking allowance before registration.

What happens after I sign up?

After registration, you choose a membership. Once it is active, you can move into the member area and continue with booking flows.
